The game is about finding a map that leads to a vault with loads of treasure but a band of cultists are trying to steal it.

You have to kill all the cultists with the millions of guns that are in the game and most of them have 2 different firing modes.

You have a choice of 4 characters which I have spoken about in my home page. And when you choose one you meet new npc characters that help you find the vault.

Bellow is one of them called Claptrap.

Claptrap gives you a tutorial on how to play the game and gives advicde on how to play the game
